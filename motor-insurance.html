Third-Party Insurance

This type of insurance is mandatory for all motor vehicle owners in Zambia.

It provides coverage for bodily injury or death of third parties and damage to their property caused by the insured vehicle.

Third-party liability insurance does not cover damage to the insured vehicle or injuries sustained by the vehicle owner.

Comprehensive Insurance

While third-party liability insurance is mandatory, comprehensive insurance is optional but highly recommended.

Comprehensive insurance provides coverage for damage to the insured vehicle, theft, and other risks not covered by third-party liability insurance.

It offers a more extensive range of protection for the policyholder's vehicle.
