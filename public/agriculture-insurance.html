Agriculture Insurance in Zambia

Agriculture insurance, also known as crop or agri-insurance, is a risk management tool that helps protect farmers from the financial losses caused by natural disasters, pests, diseases, and other unforeseen events that can negatively impact crop yields or livestock production.

In many developing countries, including Zambia, agriculture is a vital sector for the economy, providing livelihoods for a significant portion of the population. Farmers face various risks, such as unpredictable weather patterns, droughts, floods, and pest infestations, which can lead to crop failures and financial losses.
